Tickets

Ticket sales will start on Wednesday, June 26, at 4pm on this page.

In order to fit everybody's needs, the price for the tickets is essentially pay what you want. One the one side, if you are a student, unemployed, or similar we don't want the ticket price from keeping you to attend, and on the other side if you earn decent money and want to support the Rails community we want to provide you with a simple way to offer a little bit of extra support.

For a regular participant we suggest a ticket price of at least 25€. If you lack the money just pay 0€, and if you have the money maybe you want to chip in 50€ or even more. It should be noted that this is a strictly non-profit event, so all your money goes into the event, or if there should be any surplus (which we don't expect) it will go to the Ruby Berlin e.V., to further support the Ruby & Rails community.
